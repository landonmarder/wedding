Our Story

In late December 2014, Hilary, in Florida on her 2L winter break, checked her Hinge account to find she had matched with Landon. Landon, as patient as he is, agreed to wait to meet Hilary until she returned to New York. They set a date for January 27th, 2015. On the 26th, in preparation for what Mayor de Blasio warned would be "one of the largest blizzards in the history of New York City," Landon canceled. When the storm ended up being a mere dusting for a Syracuse native like Hilary, Landon called (yes, called) Hilary and asked if she still wanted to go. They met a couple of hours later at the Temple Bar in the Village.

From long distance -- Brooklyn to the Village, and then longer distance summers from Brooklyn to DC and then Brooklyn to Syracuse -- in September 2016, they finally settled (for a year) in Washington, DC where Dougie joined the family. Hilary, Landon, and Dougie returned to New York the next fall and said "we do" shortly after.

We can't wait to celebrate with you in Syracuse in November 2019!

- Hilary & Landon
